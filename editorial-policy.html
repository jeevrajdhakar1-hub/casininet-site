Editorial Policy

CASINInet News follows a clear and responsible editorial policy to ensure accuracy, fairness, and transparency in all published content. Our editorial decisions are guided by public interest and factual integrity.

1. Accuracy & Fact-Checking

We make every effort to verify information before publication. Facts are checked using official statements, publicly available data, ground inputs where applicable, and reliable media sources.

We avoid speculation and clearly distinguish between verified information and analysis.

2. Editorial Independence

CASINInet News operates as an independent digital news platform. Editorial decisions are not influenced by advertisers, sponsors, political groups, or external organizations.

3. Sources of Information

Our reporting may be based on:

  • Ground-level reporting and local inputs
  • Official government or institutional statements
  • Publicly available data and reports
  • Reliable national and international media agencies

Anonymous or unverified sources are avoided unless absolutely necessary and in the public interest.

4. International News Coverage

International news published on CASINInet News is based on reports from reliable global news agencies, official briefings, and public sources. We do not claim exclusive reporting on international events unless clearly stated.

5. Corrections Policy

If an error is identified in any published content, we correct it promptly and transparently. Readers may report errors or concerns through our official contact email.

6. No Paid or Misleading Content

CASINInet News does not publish paid news, fake stories, or misleading content. Sponsored material, if any, is clearly labeled and kept separate from editorial content.

7. Responsibility to Readers

We aim to present information in a clear, neutral, and understandable manner. Sensationalism, hate speech, and misleading narratives are strictly avoided.
