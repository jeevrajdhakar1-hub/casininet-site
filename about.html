About Us

CASINInet News is an independent digital news platform committed to delivering accurate, clear, and responsible journalism. We focus on stories that directly impact people’s lives, especially at the local and regional level.

Our coverage includes rural affairs, agriculture, local governance, social issues, and economic developments. Alongside local reporting, we also publish national and international news based on verified reports from reliable public sources and global media agencies.

CASINInet News follows a public-interest editorial approach. We aim to explain events in a simple and factual manner, avoiding sensationalism and misleading narratives.

All articles published on CASINInet News go through an internal editorial review process. Information is gathered from ground inputs, official statements, publicly available data, and reliable media reports.

CASINInet News does not publish fake news, paid news, or misleading content. If any factual error is identified, we correct it transparently and promptly.
